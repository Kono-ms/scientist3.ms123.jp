User’s Guide

STEP.1　Registration

Membership registration is required to access the site.
Membership registration is free of admission and annual fees.
*Only one account per supplier.
*Free e-mail addresses cannot be registered.

First, please enter your e-mail address and password before reading and agreeing to the Terms of Use to complete your provisional registration.

To register, you must input your basic details and complete a screening process.
You will receive an email once the reviewing process is complete.

STEP.2　Contract

To use this site, you will need to sign an agreement.

STEP.3　Looking for a service

Customers can search for a service, and if they have an interest in it, they can send an inquiry.
You can check the details of inquiries from each business chat screen via the “Opportunity List” tab.
Please reply using the chat function.

STEP.4　Quote

Once the customer decides on a service, you will receive a request for a quotation. Please upload your quotation.
If a customer has a question about the quotation terms, please reply via the chat screen.

STEP.5　Order

Please start the service only upon receipt of the order form and samples.

STEP.6　Delivery and Billing

On service provision completion, upload the report and invoice or ship the deliverables to Cosmo Bio.
Payment shall be made 60 days after the end of each month for the work completed up until the end of that month.
