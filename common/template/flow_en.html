Quick start guide

  • STEP.1

    Registration

    To use this site, you will need to register as a member.

  • STEP.2

    Contract

    To use this site, you will need to sign an agreement.

  • STEP.3

    Looking for a service

    If a customer searches for your service and is interested in your service, you will receive an inquiry. You can use the chat function to provide an answer.

  • STEP.4

    Quote

    Once the customer have decided on the service, you will receive a request for a quote.

  • STEP.5

    Order

    If the customer agrees to the terms of your quotation, a purchase order will be sent.

  • STEP.6

    Delivery and Billing

    After you have delivered the data, deliverable etc, please send an invoice.
