Quick start guide

  • STEP.1

    RegistrationIn

    Order to use this website, you need to register as a member.

  • STEP.2

    Contact

    In order to use this website, you need to sign a contract.

  • STEP.3

    Looking for a service

    Customers who are looking for a service and are interested in it will contact us. Please answer using the chat function.

  • STEP.4

    Quote

    Once the customer have decided on the service, you will receive a request for a quote. When you send in a quotation, the administrator will convert the currency and submit it to you.

  • STEP.5

    Order

    A purchase order will be sent to you once ordering client agrees to the quote.

  • STEP.6

    Delivery and Billing

    Please issue an invoice after you have delivered delivery and billing information.
