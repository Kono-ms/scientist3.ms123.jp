Outline of Scientist3

Scientist3 (Scientist Cube) is a matching platform that connects researchers and suppliers around the world, provided by Cosmo Bio, with the aim of providing science by scientists and scientists for scientists. Scientist3 is a new service developed only for Japanese researchers who can easily and efficiently use contract services around the world. It is a more user-friendly service that reflects negotiations and experience with Cosmo Bio's suppliers. Only by signing a contract with Cosmo Bio, a comprehensive contract can be made, and the services of suppliers around the world can be used without additional contracts. Cosmo Bio can also send test substances and import deliveries on your behalf, which has been difficult, so you can feel free to use our services.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

About Scientist3 What is Scientist3?

Scientist3 is a new matching platform that links suppliers and researchers. Researchers can search for services throughout the world as well as consult and negotiate online. Everything from orders to invoices can be completed on one platform.

About Scientist3 Who is Scientist3 operated by?

Scientist3 is managed by Cosmo Bio Co., Ltd.

About the use Can Scientist3 be used by anyone?

It can be used by all Japanese researchers after registration completion.
Suppliers who wish to offer their services through Scientist3 are requested to undertake a registration review.
Suppliers should register as members here.

About the use What do I need to do to use Scientist3?

Researchers must register to search for supplier services. Suppliers must register to post supplier information.
Suppliers should register as members here.

About the use Do I need to pay to use it?

It is free to become a member on Scientist3. When ordering services from a supplier via Scientist3, the client pays a usage fee that differs depending on the service cost.

About the contract Is it possible to sign a confidentiality agreement?

The Terms of Use and Terms and Conditions include confidentiality clauses.

About withdrawal from membership I want to cancel my membership. What procedures do I need to follow to do so?

Please contact us here if you would like to cancel your membership.

About payment How do I pay for the service?

You can select to use any of the yen, US dollar, or euro currencies. Suppliers will be asked to specify which currency is to be used when registering with Scientist3. The researcher pays the supplier service fees via Scientist3’s management company based on an invoice issued upon service completion.

About requests I want to delete/edit a message that I have sent.

Please contact the administrator.
