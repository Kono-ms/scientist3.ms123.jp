Scientist3 Terms of Supplier Service Agreements

Chapter 1.Purpose and Definitions

Article 1.1.Purpose; Scope of Application

The purpose of these Terms of Supplier Service Agreements (these “Terms”) is to set forth the transaction terms applicable between the Client and the Supplier, in relation to the transactions that may be entered into between the Client and the Supplier through the platform operated by Cosmo Bio Co., Ltd. (“Cosmo Bio”) under the name of “Scientist3” (the “Platform”). These Terms shall apply to all such transactions.

Article 1.2.Definitions

As used in these Terms, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth in the following items:

  • (1) “Supplier Service” means any service entrusted by the Client and accepted by the Supplier through the Platform;
  • (2) “Client” means the corporation or sole proprietor entrusting any Supplier Service;
  • (3) “Supplier” means the corporation or sole proprietor that has registered as a supplier under the Platform using the method prescribed by Cosmo Bio, and that accepts the entrustment of any Supplier Service;
  • (4) “Party” or “Parties” individually or collectively refers to the Client and the Supplier;
  • (5) “Supplier Service Agreement” means an agreement concerning any Supplier Service which is entered into between the Parties in accordance with these Terms;
  • (6) “Supplier Service Fees,” irrespective of the name thereof, collectively refers to all consideration and expenses to be paid by the Client to the Supplier in connection with any Supplier Service;
  • (7) “CB Commission Fees” means the commission fees which become payable by the Client to Cosmo Bio in connection with each transaction between the Client and the Supplier; the CB Commission Fees shall include the Platform usage fees applicable at the time of using any Supplier Service, as well as the shipping agency fees in the event of Cosmo Bio performing an agency service for shipping any Materials or receiving any Deliverables (including the operation of sending a Client deliverables received by Cosmo Bio from the Supplier);
  • (8) “Technical Information” means, among the technical information necessary for the performance of any Supplier Service (including documents or materials concerning the stability, physical/chemical properties or toxicity of the Materials, as well as other documents or materials concerning the Materials), such technical information which the Supplier has specified in the relevant Quotation/SOW (having the meaning set forth in Article 2.1, Paragraph 2; the same applies hereinafter);
  • (9) “Materials” means, among the materials to be used for any work to be performed in any Supplier Service, the materials which the Supplier has specified in the relevant Quotation/SOW;
  • (10) “Deliverables” means a report on test results, or any other document, data or item which is set forth in any Supplier Service Agreement as being the outcome of such Supplier Service;
  • (11) “Client Agreement” means the “Scientist3 Service Agreement for Clients” entered into between Cosmo Bio and the Client with respect to the Client’s use of the Platform and other matters; and
  • (12) “Supplier ToS” means the “Scientist3 Terms of Service for Suppliers” which sets forth the terms applicable to the transactions between Cosmo Bio and the Supplier.

Chapter 2.Execution of Supplier Service Agreements

Article 2.1.Placing and Accepting Orders

  • 1. The Client shall make a request to the Supplier to provide a quotation for the Supplier Service Fees concerning any desired Supplier Service, by informing the Supplier of all information necessary for such quotation via the Platform.
  • 2. Upon receiving a request for a quotation from the Client as set forth in the preceding paragraph, the Supplier shall, in line with the contents of the Supplier Service desired by the Client, prepare a written quotation in Japanese or English (a “Quotation”), and also prepare a statement of work in Japanese or English as necessary (an “SOW”; together with a Quotation, a “Quotation/SOW”). In the Quotation/SOW, the Supplier shall specify the following matters, in addition to the quotation price: (a) the details of the Supplier Service and Deliverables; (b) the delivery date; and (c) the method of shipping the Deliverables. Furthermore, the Supplier shall, as necessary, set forth in such Quotation/SOW: (d) the Technical Information and Materials to be provided by the Client; (e) the payment terms for the consideration; and (f) other information related to the performance of the Supplier Service. The Supplier must include the total amount of the Supplier Service Fees within the quotation amount.
  • 3. The Supplier shall, via the Platform, carry out the procedure for presenting the Quotation/SOW prepared in accordance with the preceding paragraph. Within three (3) business days (to be calculated according to the Japanese calendar; the same applies hereinafter) after having confirmed the completion of such procedure, Cosmo Bio shall prepare a quotation showing the sum of the Supplier Service Fees specified in the Quotation above, plus the applicable CB Commission Fees (a “CB Quotation”), and present the CB Quotation (and an SOW, in addition to the CB Quotation, in the case where the Supplier has prepared such SOW) to the Client via the Platform.
  • 4. The Client shall check the contents of the CB Quotation (and the SOW, if applicable) presented via the Platform. If the Client accepts the contents thereof, it shall carry out an ordering procedure via the Platform within the period of thirty (30) days after the date on which the CB Quotation is issued. When requesting an extension of such period, the Client shall notify the Supplier of its request for such extension by no later than three (3) business days before the expiration of such period, and obtain the Supplier’s approval for the same.
  • 5. Within three (3) business days after having confirmed the completion of the ordering procedure carried out pursuant to the preceding paragraph, Cosmo Bio shall, on the Client’s behalf, give notice to the Supplier via the Platform that an order has been placed with the Supplier.
  • 6. If the Supplier receives notice from Cosmo Bio in accordance with the preceding paragraph, a Supplier Service Agreement shall be formed between the Client and the Supplier as of such point, with the contents of their agreement being the descriptions of the aforementioned Quotation/SOW.

Article 2.2.Formation of Supplier Service Agreements

  • 1. All Supplier Service Agreements shall be formed directly between the Client and the Supplier, and Cosmo Bio shall not be a party thereto.
  • 2. The contents of each Supplier Service Agreement shall be for the Supplier to provide the relevant Supplier Service to the Client, and for the Client to pay the Supplier the relevant Supplier Service Fees as consideration therefor. The conditions of such Supplier Service Agreement shall be in accordance with the conditions set forth in Chapters 3 and 4 hereof, the conditions set forth in the relevant Quotation/SOW, and other conditions which may be individually agreed upon between the Client and the Supplier. The Supplier shall not have the obligation to comply with any request from the Client which is not included in such agreement terms. If any condition set forth in the Quotation/SOW or any other condition individually agreed upon between the Client and the Supplier is inconsistent or in conflict with any of the conditions set forth in Chapters 3 and 4 hereof, the former shall prevail.
  • 3. Notwithstanding each of the preceding paragraphs, the Client and the Supplier shall not be permitted to enter into a Supplier Service Agreement which contains any condition that is in breach of, inconsistent with or in conflict with the Client Agreement, the Supplier ToS, or any other terms, etc. prescribed by Cosmo Bio concerning the Platform. Any condition of a Supplier Service Agreement that is in breach of this paragraph shall be null and void.
  • 4. The relationship between the Supplier and the Client shall be an arm’s length relationship between an orderer and a contractor. Nothing in these Terms nor the Supplier Service Agreement appoints the Supplier as an employee, worker or agent of the Client.

The Supplier shall provide all Supplier Services in accordance with the standards, processes, delivery dates, test methods and other conditions set forth in the Quotation/SOW, and in the quality and manner that at a minimum conform to the standards in the industry of business operators providing similar services. The Supplier warrants that it has the ability to provide all Supplier Services in such quality and manner.

Cosmo Bio shall present the Client an invoice specifying the Supplier Service Fees and the CB Commission Fees (an “Invoice”) via the Platform, without delay after the completion of such procedure. The Client shall pay the amount specified in the Invoice by no later than the payment due date specified therein.
  • 3. The Client shall make payment pursuant to the preceding paragraphs by way of transfer to the bank account designated by Cosmo Bio, in the currency prescribed in the Client Agreement. All transfer fees shall be borne by the Client. Cosmo Bio shall pay the Supplier any Supplier Service Fees paid to Cosmo Bio by the Client, on the later of: (a) the sixty-fifth (65th) day after the date on which Cosmo Bio issues an Invoice thereof to the Client; or (b) the fifth (5th) business day after the date on which the Client pays the same to Cosmo Bio. Cosmo Bio’s payment of the Supplier Service Fees to the Supplier shall be made by way of transfer to the bank account which is designated by the Supplier using the method prescribed by Cosmo Bio, and shall be made in the currency prescribed in the Supplier ToS. All transfer fees shall be borne by Cosmo Bio. For the avoidance of doubt, Cosmo Bio shall not make any payment to the Supplier unless it receives payment from the relevant Client pursuant to this paragraph.
  • 4. The Client and the Supplier hereby confirm that Cosmo Bio has the authority to receive the Supplier Service Fees on behalf of the Supplier. The Client’s obligation to pay the Supplier Service Fees shall cease to exist upon the completion of payment thereof by the Client in accordance with the preceding paragraph.
  • 5. If the Supplier incurs an obligation to refund any Supplier Service Fees as a result of the relevant Supplier Service Agreement being modified or being cancelled, or for any other reason, the Supplier shall, within five (5) business days after incurring such obligation, give notice to Cosmo Bio in writing (including in the form of an electronic or magnetic record; the same applies hereinafter) to the effect that it will refund the Supplier Service Fees, and shall make such refund by way of transfer to a bank account designated by Cosmo Bio, and in the currency prescribed in the Supplier ToS. All transfer fees and other expenses which may become necessary in association with such refund shall be borne by the Supplier. The Client and the Supplier confirm that, in the event of such refund, Cosmo Bio shall have the authority to receive said refund on behalf of the Client. The Supplier’s obligation to make such refund shall cease to exist upon the completion of the payment thereof by the Supplier in accordance with this paragraph. Cosmo Bio shall pay the Client the refunded amount received from the Supplier, by way of transfer to the bank account which is designated by the Client using the method prescribed by Cosmo Bio, and shall be made in the currency prescribed in the Client Agreement. All transfer fees, as well as all expenses necessary for any currency exchange, shall be borne by the Client. For the avoidance of doubt, the CB Commission Fees shall not be refunded even in the event of any Supplier Service Fees being refunded.
