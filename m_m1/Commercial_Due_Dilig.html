Commercial Due Diligence
At Scientist.com we are committed to conducting our business responsibly,
legally and ethically, both within our organization and in all our business relationships.
Our commitments are reflected in our Code of Conduct.
This states that we work only with third parties who embrace standards of ethical behavior
that are consistent with our own.
To support this we publish our 'Global Compliance Standard' on our marketplace for reference.

To ensure we support access to the highest quality scientific research,
Scientist.com conducts extensive due diligence on all suppliers and clients utilizing the Scientist.com marketplace.
Our due diligence processes replicates or enhances the processes that currently exist within our clients and
have been designed to ensure that clients meet their internal policies and procedures when working
through the Scientist.com platform.

Benefits
Support clients in maintaining compliance with their internal policies by sharing required information proactively.
Ability to proactively update the information to ensure it's always correct.
Increase awareness of your business to Clients you don't currently work with.
