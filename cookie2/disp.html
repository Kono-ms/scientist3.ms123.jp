What is a Cookie?

A cookie is information stored on your computer, tablet, smartphone, or other devices supporting Internet. This website uses cookies is called session and persistent cookies. Session cookies are stored temporarily for the duration of your visit to this website, whereas persistent cookies are stored on your computer, tablet, smartphone, etc., even after exit the website or until you manually delete them.
